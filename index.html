GULAB JAMUN

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Baking Powder
  • Oil
  • Sugar
  • Saffron strands (optional)
  • Khoya

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Let the dough rest
  3. Prepare the sugar syrup
  4. Roll the jamuns
  5. Fry the jamuns
  6. Add jamuns to the sugar syrup
  7. Garnish

KAJU KATLI

Ingredients

  • Cashew nuts
  • Powdered sugar
  • Water
  • Ghee
  • Cardamom powder
  • Silver leaf

Steps

  1. Grind Cashew Nuts
  2. Prepare Sugar Syrup
  3. Cook Sugar Syrup
  4. Add Cashew Powder
  5. Cook and Mix
  6. Add Flavor and Ghee
  7. Roll the Dough
  8. Knead and Flatten
  9. Cut into Diamonds
  10. Decorate and Serve
